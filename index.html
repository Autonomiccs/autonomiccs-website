Project

The Autonomiccs platform is a distributed virtual machine scheduler open source software that provides the vital components needed to manage a cloud computing environment autonomously. To achieve that, we developed an autonomic plugin that can monitor the CC environment and take decisions in order to fulfill goals that are set by system administrators. This project is a plugin for the Apache CloudStack (Apache CloudStack).

Autonomiccs platform tackles dynamic, scalable and heterogeneous environments, such as cloud computing infrastructures. With that in mind, we developed a scalable architecture that enables the use of agents for different tasks; also, we designed the administration process to work upon parts of the environment, instead of acting upon the whole environment at once. Currently, the management process occurs only at the cluster level, migrating virtual machines and powering on/off idle hosts; this approach makes its processing time feasible and migrations viable without much analysis; clusters are aggregates of hosts that have the same hypervisors and CPU capabilities, which allows migrating virtual machines among any host that belongs to the same cluster.

Our agents can move workloads around, letting idle servers to power off; those agents can also be used to balance the load in the whole environment and manage the service level agreements between providers and clients.

SLA control

Migrate virtual machines before service degradation occurs as a consequence of resource competition; that can help you to manage service level agreements (SLA).

Workload balance

Make sure that hosts do not get overloaded or idle by balancing constantly the workload (virtual machines) among physical hosts.

Environmentally friendly

Save energy with heuristics that will strive to power off as many hosts as possible. This kind of heuristics can consolidate virtual machines into a smaller set of hosts, enabling the shutdown of idle ones.

About

Autonomiccs is a startup created in 2016, located at Florianópolis, Brazil. Our objectives, mission and history are presented below.

Objectives

We are committed to (i) market solutions that autonomically manage cloud computing infrastructures created with Apache CloudStack (ACS); (ii) customize our product to achieve individual needs of clients; (iii) support ACS to lead the cloud computing orchestration market; (iv) invest on academic research to improve the state of the art on autonomic management of cloud computing environments.

Mission

We do not wish to remove the need for human administrators, but to improve their work. Our mission is to optimize and make CC environments more efficient and stable. Due to our academic background, we recognize that investing in research is vital to develop complex solutions; such as the autonomic management of cloud computing infrastructures. Thus, our goal is to integrate research with cloud computing companies needs.

History

Autonomiccs was created to fill the lack of autonomic systems embedded in cloud computing orchestration platforms. The idea was born in 2014, but the hard work started at 2015.

  • 2013-2014

    Research.

    A lot of discussion around important topics and the research that helped us to understand better the cloud computing management topic happened at this time. Also, this period was crucial as it was deployed a cloud with the Apache CloudStack in our lab.

  • February 2015

    A project was Born.

    The idea (the seed) that we have been discussed for a long time has just been planted and started growing. At this point we started to formalize our first component that came lately to be the Autonomiccs platform core.

  • October 2015

    The cloud became autonomic.

    The first prototype is integrated with our cloud environment, that cloud was orchestrated with Apache CloudStack 4.3.1.

  • April 2016

    Hello world!

    The project was ported to ACS 4.6.0 (and beyond). The code was opened to the world and the project launched. Time to spread the word about our solution to companies and CloudStack community members.

Custom made agents

The Autonomiccs platform has a set of agents working with different tasks. We can develop new agents to cover your specific needs.

Multi-agent systems are an aggregation of agents that perform simple tasks in order to achieve the fulfillment of more complex ones. Each agent can act upon a different aspect of the environment, enabling the division of labor; for instance, we can have different agents, each one focused on dealing with a distinct hypervisor or components of the CC environment.

Some tasks may request the creation of new agents to perform it. We offer our background to develop new agents that tackle your peculiar issues.

Heuristics development

One of the Autonomiccs platform main characteristics is that it can attend different goals; that is achieved through the use of heuristics.

We had already developed a set of algorithms that seek to achieve some specific goals; e.g. (i) reduce the energy consumption by migrating VMs workloads and powering off idle hosts; (ii) distribute VMs among hosts to balance resources usage. Although they are good to develop and test our plugin, these heuristics are the result of a technology in early development and cannot cover all scenarios.

Heuristics are designed to guide our plugin decisions, defining target hosts to be migrated, which virtual machine can be migrated, if a host can be powered off, which host to power off, preferences of hosts to activate, and so on. Designing a brand new heuristic is not an easy task, it needs to be formalized, tested and well planned to achieve the desired goal. With that in mind, we offer our background to develop heuristics towards our clients specific needs.
