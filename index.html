On-Demand Services

Have all the benefits of the Autonomiccs platform in your environment on a pay-as-you-manage basis.

Never again will you worry about managing and optimizing your cloud system. Delegate that exhausting job to our platform instead; they will make the most out of your cloud's resources through its API.

How it Works

In a nutshell, our agents gather data about your cloud through its API, analyzing the state of the environment to decide on what actions to take to satisfy your demands for optimization, be it consolidating the most VMs into the least physical hosts or balancing them so as to avoid conflicts for resources and service degradation. Those decisions are based on a set of heuristics that you can choose. To track our agents and their actions we've created an intuitive interface that shows the current placement of VMs, resource allocation and usage.

It's easy to use!

Only a few steps to an autonomic cloud:

  1. Enter your CloudStack Admin keys (API/secret) into our login page;

  2. Choose a management algorithm from our presets (or plug in your own!);

  3. Enjoy!



SLA control

Migrate virtual machines before service degradation occurs as a consequence of resource competition; this can help you manage service level agreements (SLA).

Workload balance

Makes sure that hosts become neither overloaded nor idle by balancing the workload (virtual machines) constantly among physical hosts.

Environmentally friendly

Save energy with heuristics that strive to power off as many hosts as possible. These heuristics can consolidate virtual machines into a smaller set of hosts, allowing idle ones to be shut down.

Project

The Autonomiccs Project consists of two different pieces of software: the Autonomiccs Management Platform, available as a service on this website, and the Autonomiccs Research Platform, available on GitHub.

The Autonomiccs Management Platform is available exclusively as a service, through this website. It is developed in a way that allows you to visualize your cloud environment and gain some insight on how its resources are being allocated and used, as well as to apply different management techniques to it, on demand.

The Autonomiccs Research platform is an open source, distributed virtual machine scheduler software that manages a cloud computing environment autonomously. This platform is available as a plugin for the Apache CloudStack™ orchestrator.

The Autonomiccs Project tackles dynamic, scalable and heterogeneous environments, such as cloud computing infrastructures. With that in mind, we developed an architecture that enables the use of agents for different tasks; we also designed the administration process to work upon parts of the environment, instead of acting upon the whole environment at once.

Our agents can move workloads around, allowing idle servers to power off; those agents can also be used to balance the load throughout the whole environment. The very same platform can be used to manage the service level agreements between providers and clients.
