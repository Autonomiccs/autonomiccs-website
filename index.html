Project

The Autonomiccs platform is an open source, distributed virtual machine scheduler software that manages a cloud computing environment autonomously. This project is a plugin for the (Apache CloudStack™)platform.

The Autonomiccs platform tackles dynamic, scalable and heterogeneous environments, such as cloud computing infrastructures. With that in mind, we developed an architecture that enables the use of agents for different tasks; we also designed the administration process to work upon parts of the environment, instead of acting upon the whole environment at once.

Our agents can move workloads around, allowing idle servers to power off; those agents can also be used to balance the load throughout the whole environment. The very same platform can be used to manage the service level agreements between providers and clients.

SLA control

Migrate virtual machines before service degradation occurs as a consequence of resource competition; this can help you manage service level agreements (SLA).

Workload balance

Makes sure that hosts become neither overloaded nor idle by balancing the workload (virtual machines) constantly among physical hosts.

Environmentally friendly

Save energy with heuristics that strive to power off as many hosts as possible. These heuristics can consolidate virtual machines into a smaller set of hosts, allowing idle ones to be shut down.

About

Autonomiccs is a startup created in 2016, located in Florianópolis, Brazil. Our objectives, mission and history are presented below.

Objectives

We are committed to (i) market solutions that autonomically manage cloud computing infrastructures created with Apache CloudStack™ (ACS); (ii) customize our product to achieve individual needs of clients; (iii) support ACS to lead the cloud computing orchestration market; (iv) invest on academic research to improve the state of the art on autonomic management of cloud computing environments.

Mission

We do not wish to remove the need for human administrators, but to improve their work. Our mission is to optimize and make cloud computing environments more efficient and stable. Due to our academic background, we recognize that investing in research is vital to develop complex solutions, such as the autonomic management of cloud computing infrastructures. Thus, our goal is to integrate cutting-edge research with cloud computing companies’ needs.

History

Autonomiccs was created to fill the lack of autonomic systems embedded in cloud computing orchestration platforms. The idea was born in 2014, but the hard work started in 2015.

  • 2013-2014

    Research.

    A lot of discussion around important topics and the research that helped us to better understand the cloud computing management topic happened at this time. Also, this period was crucial as it was when a cloud was deployed in our lab using Apache CloudStack™.

  • February 2015

    A project was Born.

    The idea (the seed) which we had been discussing for a long time has just been planted and started growing. At this point, we started to formalize our first component which eventually became the Autonomiccs platform core.

  • October 2015

    The cloud became autonomic.

    The first prototype is integrated with our cloud environment, which was orchestrated using Apache CloudStack™ 4.3.1.

  • April 2016

    Hello world!

    The project was ported to ACS 4.6.0 (and beyond). The code was opened to the world and the project launched. Time to spread the word about our solution to companies and CloudStack® community members.

Cloud Management and Optimization as a Service

Have all the benefits and more of Autonomiccs platform in your environment without needing to deploy and maintain it.

Using this service you do not need to worry about the management and optimization of your cloud system. You can let that exhausting job to our agents; they will manage your cloud through its API. Moreover, agents never get tired, do not have moods swing, and strive to make the most to attend your needs.

How does it work?

In a nutshell, our agents gather data about your cloud through its API, and then they analyze its state and decide which virtual machines migrations have to be executed and physical hosts that can be powered on/off. Those decisions are based on a set of heuristics that you can choose. To track our agents and their actions we created an interface that shows placement of VMs, resource allocation and usage.

It is simple to use!

Few steps for an autonomic cloud:

  1. Provide the CloudStack Admin keys (API/secret);

  2. Choose some heuristic from our preset, or plug heuristics customized by yourself;

  3. Enjoy!

Custom made agents

Some cloud environments may have different needs, which might require the development of new agents. We offer our background to develop new agents that tackle your particular issues.

Multi-agent systems are an aggregation of agents that perform simple tasks in order to achieve the fulfillment of more complex ones. Each agent can act upon a different aspect of the environment, enabling the division of labor; for instance, we can have different agents, each one focused on dealing with a distinct hypervisor or component of the cloud environment.

Some tasks may require the creation of new agents to be performed. We offer our background to develop new agents that may tackle your particular issues.

Heuristics development

One of the Autonomiccs platform's main characteristics is that it can reach different goals.

We already developed a set of algorithms that seek to achieve some specific goals; e.g. (i) reduce the energy consumption by migrating workloads of VMs into fewer servers and powering off idle ones; (ii) distribute VMs among hosts to balance resources usage. Although they are good to develop and test our plugin, these heuristics are the result of a technology in early development and cannot suffice all possible needs.

Heuristics are designed to guide our plugin decisions, defining target hosts to be migrated, which virtual machine can be migrated, if a host can be powered off, which hosts to power off, preferences of hosts to activate, and so on. Designing a brand new heuristic is not an easy task, it needs to be formalized, tested and well planned to achieve the desired goal. With that in mind, we offer our background to develop heuristics towards our clients' specific needs.
